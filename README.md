# MedTerm Easy Explainer 🩺

## a. App Overview
* **App Name:** MedTerm Easy Explainer
* **Problem Solved:** Medical terminology and lab test terms are often hard for beginners and students to quickly grasp. This app translates complex medical terms into simple, easy-to-understand explanations.
* **Target Audience:** Medical students and healthcare beginners.

## b. Live Deployed URL
* **Live App:** https://yourusername.github.io/medterm-easy-explainer/

## c. Features List
* Interactive web form to input medical terms.
* Instant simple summary response.
* Direct integration with Google Gemini AI.

## d. AI Feature & System Prompt
* **AI Feature:** Takes complex terms and breaks them down into plain English.
* **System Prompt:** `"You are an expert medical teacher explaining terms to beginners. Explain the given medical term or lab result in 3 extremely short, clear, bullet points using plain language."`

## e. Tools & Models Used

* **Google Gemini 1.5 Flash API**
